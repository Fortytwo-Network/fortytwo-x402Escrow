# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free, and collaborative environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Welcome feedback and different viewpoints.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or deliberate disruption.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate this code of conduct.

For reports, contact:
- `support@fortytwo.network`
